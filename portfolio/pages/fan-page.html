My Coffee Hobby

I like drinking coffee, and I really like the pour over method of brewing coffee. So it has become a hobby of mine to brew really good pour over coffee at home.

The Water

For me It starts with the water. It must be clean and hardened with the right minerals. Natural spring water, mineral water or a water additive will do the trick.

The Beans

So far I've been using a lot of off-the-shelf coffee grinds. But soon I will start using fresh roasted beans from a quality roaster.
